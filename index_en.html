What is MissionForge?

The Problem — By Example

Imagine: You have a process catalog with 40 processes. You want to turn it into interactive documentation where each process is shown as an editable workflow.

If you give this to an AI as one big task: The first 15 processes look great, then it loses track. Process 23 gets missed, formats don't match, quality checks are absent.

The Solution: From Chaos to Company

📄 Process Catalog PDF
→
🏗️ MissionForge
→
✅ 40 Interactive Workflows

MissionForge breaks this task into pieces, assigns each piece to a specialized agent, and verifies at the end that all 40 processes are complete and consistent.

What MissionForge Does for You

🏢
Company Spawning
From your task, a complete organization with teams, agents, and rules is created automatically.
🎯
Nothing Gets Lost
All 40 processes are captured, assigned, and checked against a checklist at the end.
⚡
Parallel Waves
Independent processes (e.g. Procurement + HR) run simultaneously — up to 3 in parallel.
📦
Reusable
New process catalog next quarter? Same mission, new document — done.
💡Key insight: MissionForge isn't a process viewer — it's the project manager ensuring 40 agents work on 40 processes and none is forgotten.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need programming skills?▼

No. Describe your task in natural language — just like the process catalog example.

What about very large documents?▼

MissionForge scales: more processes = more waves. Beyond 25+ packages, it splits into missions.

Can I change the plan before execution?▼

Yes. Phase 5 shows the full wave plan. Nothing moves until you approve.

What if I need this again next quarter?▼

Export as .skill. Next time: "Run process-catalog.skill for [new PDF]".

What are the prerequisites?▼

Claude Code or compatible agent runtime. Tools: Bash, Read, Write, Edit.
